atkinson landscaping

visual identity + branding

Atkinson Landscaping was a booming business with a weak visual identity. Run by two young entrepreneurs with a passion for excellent customer experience, the brand didnâ€™t reflect the vision or personality of the company. Landscaping companies can be untrustworthy and under-qualified, and so the owners approached me for a brand-new bold and recognizable logo supported by professional-grade brand deliverables.

the old

I actually had previously designed a logo for Atkinson Landcaping when they first started up, but made two critical errors in that design: first, in an effort to go retro, I used a script font as the focal point. Because of the looping script, the logo could be read as "Allison Landscaping". Second, the secondary font that I chose was too light of a weight, making it practically invisible. Both of these decisions eventually caused the owners to go to a different designer and use a different logo.

The second time they asked me to work with them, I was not going to make those mistakes again.

the new

Starting with a clean and legible font, I wanted to create a logo that had distinct features which could be seen and identified from a distance--especially from a car passing by a company truck or yard sign. The green-blue analogous colour scheme is memorable, and can be associated with an Earth-sky relationship. Through hours of pixel-by-pixel feedback, the completed logo was as much a labour of love for the owners as it was for me.

"Rebecca helped us to create a great logo and brand that is now highly recognized in the community."

Matt Atkinson, owner

now we here

Designing business cards, car magnets, and t-shirts for Atkinson Landscaping has generated a lot of brand recognition in the community since we started the rebrand two years ago. The new identity has shifted the owners' focus from just running a functioning company, to casting a larger company vision of being the best, most respected landscaping company in the Lower Mainland. The company found new life and a new swagger.

"Rebecca built our brand. She took our dreams and made them into reality."

Dave Endacott, owner
